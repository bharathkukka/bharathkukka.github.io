Slide 1: Title


The 5W1H Framework

A Visual Guide to Asking the Right Questions

Slide 2: What is 5W1H?


A framework for gathering complete information.

Each question is a piece of the puzzle. Answering them all gives you the full picture.

Slide 3: WHO?


Who is involved?

This question identifies the people.


Slide 4: WHAT?


What is the subject?

This question defines the action or event.


Slide 5: WHERE?


Where is it happening?

This question establishes the location.


Slide 6: WHEN?


When is the time?

This question provides the timeframe.


Slide 7: WHY?


Why is it happening?

This question uncovers the reason and purpose.


Slide 8: HOW?


How will it be done?

This question explains the process and method.


Slide 9: Conclusion


From Confusion to Clarity

Using the 5W1H framework ensures you never miss a critical detail.
